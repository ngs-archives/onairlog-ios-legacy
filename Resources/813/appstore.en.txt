This software provides informations of aired songs on J-WAVE FM ( http://www.j-wave.co.jp/ ) , Tokyo, Japan.
Updates most recent aired songs; Searches back to the past.

Songs which sold on the iTunes Store are avail to preview and buy.

You can check them later with Bookmarks.
